Get Help

If you or someone you know is struggling with mental health, please reach out to a mental health professional or call a helpline.

In the U.S., you can reach the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline at 1-800-273-TALK (1-800-273-8255).

It's important to talk to someone who can help.
